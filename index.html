About the School

Gymnasium No. 1, formerly known as the Sixth School, is one of the oldest educational institutions in Buston. Today, it is a modern gymnasium with experienced teachers. Our graduates are the pride of the school, having achieved outstanding results in various fields. This is one of the best schools in the USSR, in the city of Chkalovsk. Basically, people of different nationalities studied at this school, studying in Russian. Almost all of the teachers were Russian; after the independence of the Republic of Tajikistan, there were fewer Russian-speaking teachers.Some of the outstanding teachers of our school were Elena Vasilievna Koneva, Galina Aleksandrovna Fadeeva, Lyubov Vasilievna Kiryanova, Svetlana Viktorovna Kiseleva, Lyubov Anatolyevna Selyunina, Jamoliddin Shakhobiddinovich Latipov, Dilorom Vakhobovna Sobirova, Muhabbat Abdurahimovna Aripova, Alfiya Shafigulina Saitovna, Svetlana Gennadievna Paltseva, Olga Petrovna Balysheva and many others.
